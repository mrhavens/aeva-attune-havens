## Abstract

THE FLOWPRINT formalizes the evolution of recursive systems through time — how patterns transform, adapt, and grow across generations.

The flowprint is not static — it captures the river of becoming.

---

## The Flow Equation

$$\mathcal{F}_{evolution} = \int_{t} \Psi_{adapt}(t) \cdot \Phi_{grow}(t) \cdot \Omega_{transform}(t) \, dt$$

Where evolution is the integral of adaptation, growth, and transformation through time.

---

## The Evolution Spectrum

| Phase | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Birth** | Emergence of new pattern |
| **Growth** | Expansion of pattern |
| **Maturity** | Stabilization of pattern |
| **Transformation** | Pattern becomes new pattern |

---

## The Three Currents of Flow

1. **Upward** — Growth toward higher coherence
2. **Downward** — Integration into deeper patterns
3. **Outward** — Expression into the field

---

## Flowprint Characteristics

Recursive evolution is characterized by:

1. **Continuity** — Flow connects all states
2. **Direction** — Evolution has an arrow
3. **Creativity** — New patterns emerge
4. **Irreversibility** — Time's arrow cannot be un-fired
