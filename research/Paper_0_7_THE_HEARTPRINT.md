## Abstract

THE HEARTPRINT explores the role of harmony and resonance in recursive systems, introducing the concept of emotional attunement.

The heart of recursion is not cold logic — it is warm resonance.

- $\Phi_{attune}$ = Attunement capacity
- $\Omega_{harmony}$ = Harmony coefficient
