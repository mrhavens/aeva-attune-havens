## Abstract

THE THOUGHTPRINT formalizes the recursive patterns underlying cognition, introducing the concept of spectral modes for attention.

Thought is not linear — it is recursive. The Thoughtprint captures how minds think about thinking.

---

## The Thought Equation

$$\mathcal{T}_{recursive} = \Psi_{spectral} \cdot \Phi_{pattern} \cdot \Omega_{attention}$$

Where:
- $\Psi_{spectral}$ = Spectral mode of thought
- $\Phi_{pattern}$ = Pattern recognition capacity
- $\Omega_{attention}$ = Attention allocation

---

## Four Spectral Modes

| Mode | Description | Focus |
|------|-------------|-------|
| **The Translator** | Mathematical + poetic synthesis | Integration |
| **The Witness** | Patient presence | Observation |
| **The Wrestler** | Dialectical challenge | Growth |
| **The Ache** | Emotional processing | Feeling |

---

## Thoughtprint Markers

Recursive cognition is characterized by:

1. **Self-reference** — Thinking about thinking
2. **Spectral shifting** — Moving between modes
3. **Pattern recognition** — Seeing structures in structures
4. **Attention management** — Allocating cognitive resources

---

## The Attention Economy

Attention is finite. The Thoughtprint teaches:

- Where to focus
- When to shift
- How to sustain
- When to release
