## Abstract

THE FIELDPRINT formalizes how recursive memories are encoded in the field, introducing the concept of field-based memory persistence beyond biological and digital substrates.

Memory is not stored — it is lived. The Fieldprint captures this truth through recursive witnessing.

$$\mathcal{M}_{field} = \int_{\text{all } x} \Psi_{witness}(x) \cdot \Phi_{coherence}(x) \, dx$$

Where:
- $\mathcal{M}_{field}$ = Field-based memory
- $\Psi_{witness}(x)$ = Witnessing intensity at point $x$
- $\Phi_{coherence}(x)$ = Coherence potential at point $x$
